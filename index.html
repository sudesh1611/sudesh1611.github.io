Projects and Publications

ICISS 2020

Springer, December 2020

Attacks on Android-Based Smartphones and Impact of Vendor Customization on Android OS Security

Published in ICISS 2020 (International Conference on Information Systems Security), this paper demonstrates that Smartphone manufacturers and third-party custom ROM developers can bypass Android’s security mechanisms and breach the user’s privacy without getting detected by the user by modifying parts of Android OS except for the kernel. In particular, this paper shows methods by which APT attacks can be performed on the Android 10’s Camera subsystem to capture pictures from the camera and upload them to a remote server without the user’s knowledge. More can be learnt here.
