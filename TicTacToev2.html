TicTacToe

It is a Xamarin.Forms application implementing the famous game "TicTacToe". This app contains cool features like amazing animations, awesome material themes, and much more. It features Three Modes:

  • Single Player
  • Two Players - Online
  • Two Players - Offline

Single Player Mode: It contains option to choose between Easy Bot or Hard Bot. Hard Bot implements Alpha–beta pruning algorithm and Easy Bot implements a greedy algorithm.

Two Players Mode - Online: In this mode, one can play with other players online . This mode consumes APIs exposed by TicTacToe Website for playing online with other players.

Two Players Mode - Offline: In this mode, one can play with others face to face .
